Tell your story

Wavenumber is a “full-stack” product design and communication organization. We combine content, industrial design, and engineering to tell product stories. Our clients benefit by having a trusted partner to both develop and communicate their product. We understand the importance of the "why" as well as the "how", aligning engineering development with sales and marketing strategies.

We value real, honest relationships and take our work seriously. Successful outcomes are achieved through application of our "full-stack" values.


Create

Understand that you literally become what you think about. Bring to the world that which is not. Move toward how we want our things to be. Take action, collaborate and build teams. Iterate and refine. Be resilient and persevere.

Communicate

Tell stories about the “why” and connect to a purpose. Freely give what we know and embrace a mindset of generosity. Actively listen and be ready to take the role of student and teacher. Become comfortable with being vulnerable.

Serve

Be your best by helping others be their best. Cultivate empathy and express gratitude. Give more than you ever expect to receive. Lead through service to others. Enable and empower others.


When we are doing these three things, everyone wins.
